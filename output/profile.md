### Executive Summary
Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive who has been the CEO of Google since 2015 and the CEO of its parent company Alphabet Inc. since 2019. He is known for his leadership style that combines technical acumen with emotional intelligence, and for championing diversity and open dialogue in the corporate environment.

- **Nationality**: Indian-American
- **Current Role / Occupation**: CEO of Alphabet and Google
- **Industry**: Technology
- **Current City and Country**: Not Publicly Available

### Biography
Sundar Pichai was born on June 10, 1972, in Madras (now Chennai), India. He grew up in a modest home in Chennai and showed an early interest in technology. According to his profile on [Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/profile/sundar-pichai), Pichai's journey to becoming the CEO of Alphabet and Google is a testament to his determination and hard work.

### Career Timeline
