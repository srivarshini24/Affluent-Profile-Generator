### Executive Summary
Sundar Pichai is a renowned Indian-born American executive, currently serving as the CEO of Google and Alphabet. Under his leadership, Google has been focused on developing products and services powered by the latest advances in AI. He has played a crucial role in the development of Google's popular products, including Google Chrome, Google Toolbar, and Google Apps.

### Basic Details
- Full Name: Sundararajan Pichai
- Nationality: Indian-American
- Current Role / Occupation: CEO of Google and Alphabet
- Industry: Technology
- Current City and Country: Not Publicly Available

### Biography
Sundar Pichai was born on June 10, 1972, in Madras (now Chennai), India. He showed an early interest in technology and pursued a career in the field. According to Britannica, Pichai grew up with limited access to technology but was always interested in how it can create community.

### Career Timeline
- 2004: Joined Google as the head of product management and development
- 2014: Appointed to lead product and engineering for all of Google’s products and platforms
- 2015: Became Google’s CEO
- 2017: Joined the Board of Directors of Alphabet, Google's parent company
- 2019: Became the CEO of Alphabet

### Education

- Master’s degree in engineering from Stanford University
- MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania
